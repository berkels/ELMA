ELMA - ELectron Micrograph Analysis

ELMA is a small tool for denoising and analyzing High-Resolution STEM images. Its main application are low-dose micrographs that are extremely corrupted Poisson noise. Currently it is focused on periodic crystals, but will be extended to more complex structures in the future.

ELMA can read/save TIFF images, as well as some other formats: read: .tiff, .tif, .dm3, .dm4, .png, .q2bz write: .tiff, .tif, .png, .q2bz
